The Story Behind AMARA

As I sat down to reflect on the journey of building AMARA, I find myself deeply moved by the stories, strength and resilience of countless women who have shaped not just this brand, but my very purpose behind creating it.

Growing up in India, I was no stranger to the silence and stigma that often surrounds menstruation. It was something we whispered about, something that came with shame, discomfort, and a sense of inconvenience. I saw how young girls were often unprepared, misinformed, or even excluded from daily life during their periods — not because they were unwell, but simply because society conditioned them to feel that way.

Isha Qureshi

Founder of AMARA

Our Mission

At AMARA, our mission is to revolutionize period care by providing innovative, organic sanitary pads that empower women to live life on their terms. We strive to break taboos surrounding menstruation, promote sustainable practices, and ensure that every woman has access to healthy and eco-friendly menstrual solutions.

Empowerment

We believe in empowering women to take control of their health and well-being.

Sustainability

We are committed to creating products that are not only good for you but also for the planet. Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve our products and services.
