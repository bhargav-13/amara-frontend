Our Brand Story

At Amara, we believe that every woman deserves a period care experience that is safe, gentle, and kind to the body. Our journey started with one mission in mind - to create pads that are soft on skin, provide protection, and kind to the planet.

Our Vision

At AMARA, our mission is to revolutionize period care by providing innovative, organic sanitary pads that empower women to live life on their terms. We aim to break taboos surrounding menstruation, promote sustainable practices, and ensure that every woman has access to healthy and eco-friendly menstrual solutions.

Sustainability Practices

We care deeply about the Earth we live on. That's why we've made it our goal to reduce waste and support a cleaner future. We use certified organic cotton grown without harmful pesticides. Our packaging is minimal and made from recycled materials with a low carbon footprint.
