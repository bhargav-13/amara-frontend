Our Brand Story

At Amara, we believe that every woman deserves a period care experience that is safe, gentle, and worry-free. Our journey started with one mission: to create sanitary pads that are soft on skin, strong on protection, and kind to the planet. We saw that many traditional pads are made with synthetic materials and chemicals that can cause discomfort, rashes, or even health concerns. That's why we created Amara – a brand that truly cares about women's wellness and the environment.

Our Vision

At AMARA, our mission is to revolutionize period care by providing innovative, organic sanitary pads that empower women to live life on their terms. We strive to break taboos surrounding menstruation, promote sustainable practices, and ensure that every woman has access to healthy and eco-friendly menstrual solutions.

Sustainability Practices

We care deeply about the Earth we live on. That's why we've made it our goal to reduce waste and support a cleaner future. We use certified organic cotton grown without harmful pesticides. Our packaging is recyclable and minimal to reduce our footprint. All our products are cruelty-free and dermatologically tested. We support eco-education and period awareness through our blog and community.
