Privacy Policy

What Information We Collect

  • We collect personal details such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information when you place an order or contact us.
  • We may automatically collect website usage data like your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • We use your information to process and deliver your orders smoothly.
  • We use it to communicate updates, special offers, and important information.
  • Your information helps us improve our products, services, and website.
  • We also use it to comply with legal requirements and protect our rights.

Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • We only share your data with trusted partners who assist with order fulfillment, payment processing, or marketing — and they must keep your information confidential.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • We retain your personal information only as long as needed to provide our services, meet legal obligations, and resolve any issues.

Children's Privacy

  • Our products and website are not intended for children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can remove it.

How We Protect Your Data

  • We use advanced security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure
