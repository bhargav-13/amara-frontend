Why Choose Organic?

Discover the benefits of organic period products for your health and the environment.

What You Put In Your Body Matters

The average woman uses over 11,000 tampons or pads in her lifetime. These products come in direct contact with one of the most sensitive and absorbent parts of your body.

Conventional sanitary products often contain synthetic materials, chemical additives, artificial fragrances, and traces of pesticides & substances you wouldn't want absorbed into your bloodstream.

Amara's organic cotton products are free from these harmful substances, providing a safer, more comfortable period experience while reducing your exposure to potentially harmful chemicals.

Health Benefits of Switching to Organic

Reduced Irritation and Allergic Reactions

Without synthetic materials, fragrances, and chemicals, organic cotton products significantly reduce the risk of skin irritation, rashes, and allergic reactions.

Better Breathability

Organic cotton allows air to circulate, reducing moisture build up and creating an environment less conducive to infections and discomfort.

No Exposure to Harmful Chemicals

Conventional products can contain traces of dioxins, pesticides, and other chemicals that may disrupt hormones and pose health risks with long-term exposure.

Reduced Risk of TSS

Some studies suggest that organic cotton products may carry a lower risk of Toxic Shock Syndrome compared to highly absorbent synthetic materials.

Environmental Impact

The Problem with Conventional Products

  • The average woman uses 11,000+ disposable period products in her lifetime.
  • Conventional pads can contain up to 90% plastic and take 500-800 years to decompose.
  • Conventional cotton production uses 16% of the world's insecticides and 7% of pesticides.
  • The manufacturing process for conventional products often involves chlorine bleaching, which creates harmful dioxins.

How Amara Makes a Difference

  • Our products are made from biodegradable materials that break down naturally.
  • Organic cotton farming uses 91% less water and 62% less energy than conventional cotton.
  • Organic cotton farming uses 91% less water and 62% less energy than conventional cotton.
  • We use oxygen whitening instead of chlorine bleach, eliminating dioxin production.
